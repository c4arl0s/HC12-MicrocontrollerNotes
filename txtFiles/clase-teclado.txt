            BRA SECDEC

      LDAA PORTA

      LDAA BANTEC

      BNE REGTEC
      INC REGTEC
      CLR BANTEC
      DEC BANTEC ; BANTEC=FF
      BRA SALTEC

      BEQ RUTEC2
      CLR BANTEC
